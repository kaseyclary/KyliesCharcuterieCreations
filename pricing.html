Pricing

Board prices start around $20 per person. Pricing varies based on where you are located. No payment is needed to reserve your date, payment is made upon arrival. I accept Cash, Check, PayPal, Facebook Pay and Venmo (card coming soon!).

Group minimum is typically 6, but feel free to submit an inquiry for less. Fill out the order form and I will be in contact with a quote!

Below is my base pricing without delivery factored in. All my boards include a variety of high end meats, cheeses, pickled items, spreads, jams, fruits, complimentary items, sweets, crackers and more. Boards can be used as an appetizer or as the main dish - this all depends on how much your group can eat! If you are requesting a board for a Holiday, there will also be a slight increase for Holiday pricing.

Group of 6-8:
$120, plus delivery

Group of 8-10:
$160, plus delivery

Group of 10-15:
$210, plus delivery

Group of 15+ message me for pricing!

Event Catering:
Event catering also varies based on guest count, location and if you are wanting to provide appetizers for guests, or have Charcuterie be the main meal.
